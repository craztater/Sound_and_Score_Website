How to Prepare Your Files

Sending properly organized stems saves time and means a better mix. Here's what I need:

Stems

— All tracks exported as individual WAV files (24-bit / 48kHz)
— No master bus processing — no limiter or heavy compression on the stereo out
— All tracks starting from 0:00 / bar 1 so everything lines up
— Label tracks clearly (Kick, Snare, Lead Vox, Guitar L, etc.)
— Note any effects or plugins you want preserved

The form on the right will ask for your reference track links, vibe, and project notes. If you're booking a mix, BPM and key fields will also appear. Fill in what you know — it doesn't need to be perfect.

Producer Composer Mix Engineer Saxophonist Songwriter

Hey! I'm Taylor. I'm a composer, producer, saxophonist, and mix engineer based in Los Angeles, and I've been obsessing over sound since before I knew what a DAW was. My catalog spans pop, EDM, rock, punk, indie, jazz, lo-fi, classical, and experimental electronic, which means I don't just work in one world: I work in yours.

I studied Jazz Saxophone at Cal State Northridge, interned at Teragram Ballroom, Moroccan Lounge, and Ice Cream Sound Recording Studios (all in Los Angeles), and have scored short films under my own name and release original music and game soundtracks under the artist name ASH3R. Film credits include Jo Super, Deer God, and The Imon Chronicles. Game OSTs: The Time Warrior and Night Shift Driver. Full catalog production for artist Xayvion.

When we work together, you're working directly with me — no middleman, no template chain. Just your vision, my ears, and someone who genuinely cares about getting it right.
